About This Project

What is this?

This is a revolutionary counter application that has been scientifically proven to count numbers with 99.9% accuracy. Our team of elite mathematicians spent countless hours perfecting the increment and decrement algorithms.

Our Mission

To provide the world's most advanced counting solution, one click at a time. We believe that everyone deserves access to premium counting technology, which is why we've made this application completely free (because we're basically philanthropists).

Key Features

  • Counts up with unprecedented precision
  • Also counts down (a technological breakthrough)
  • Reset button for when you've counted too much
  • Remembers your count even after you close the browser (thanks to cutting-edge localStorage technology)
  • Beautiful gradient that makes counting 47% more enjoyable
  • Keyboard shortcuts for power users
